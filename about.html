About Me

I am an emerging front-end developer and software engineer currently in an exciting, hands-on learning phase of my tech journey. I focus on building clean, interactive, and user-centric web applications while continuously expanding my technical toolkit. My path into tech is fueled by a mix of logical problem-solving and creative design, meaning I care just as much about robust backend functionality and semantic code as I do about crafting flawless, responsive layouts using tools like CSS Grid and Flexbox.

As an active learner, I thrive on taking concepts from tutorials and turning them into real, live projects. Every bug I troubleshoot and every layout I build is a step toward refining my craft. When I am not writing functions or debugging styles, you can find me gaming, playing the piano, or getting some well-deserved sleep.

I am always looking to connect with mentors, fellow learners, and tech professionals to collaborate on innovative web projects. Let's build and learn together, feel free to reach out!
